Who I Am

"I am a developer, a creative, and relentlessly curious person" - Shannan Gonser

I am a developer that focuses on FrontEnd Web developement because I enjoy working with visuals the most. So this seemed like the natural path for me. As I progress through my developer journey I do plan on exploring backend pursuits to become a formidable Full-Stack Developer.

I have a Bachelors in Computer Science where I studied various programming languages, Software Design & Development, Data Structures, Algorithms and more. During my formal educational journey, I had the liberty of gaining experience in cooperating with different individuals that were "non-techies" and "techies". I found this beneficial for me because it allowed me to continuously improve my communication skills when I need to explain the tough topics as well as improve my own knowledge of the topics. In short, this is me saying that I just genuinely enjoy working with others.

I plan on continuing to further my skills and knowledge as well as growing with others on my journey.
